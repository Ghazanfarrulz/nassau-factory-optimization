FACTORY REALLOCATION & SHIPPING OPTIMIZATION
Nassau Candy Distributor

Project Objective
This project develops a decision-support system that predicts shipping lead time, compares alternative factories, and recommends product reallocations while balancing operational speed and profitability.

Core Capabilities
• Cleans and prepares order, shipping, product, region, sales, cost, and profit data.
• Calculates shipping lead time from order and ship dates.
• Maps products to their current manufacturing factories.
• Compares Linear Regression, Random Forest, and Gradient Boosting models.
• Simulates each product across five possible factories.
• Produces lead-time reduction, profit impact, risk, confidence, and recommendation scores.
• Provides an interactive Streamlit dashboard and downloadable results.

Business Value
The system helps leadership test factory-reallocation scenarios before operational execution. It highlights potential shipping improvements, identifies financial risks, and provides a ranked list of alternatives under user-selected speed-versus-profit priorities.

Data Limitation
The dataset does not provide real freight charges, customer coordinates, factory capacity, or historical reassignment results. These outputs are therefore scenario-based estimates and should be validated with operational data before implementation.
